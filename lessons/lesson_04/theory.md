| --- | --- |
| `/bin` | **Essential binaries** (commands) needed for basic system use (e.g. `ls`, `cp`, `mv`, `bash`) ‚Äî available to all users. |

| `/dev` | Virtual files representing **hardware devices** (e.g. `/dev/sda` for disks, `/dev/null`). |
